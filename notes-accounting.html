📘 Accounting — Double Entry & Financial Statements

Every transaction affects at least two accounts — debit and credit. We prepare a trial balance to ensure accuracy before creating the financial statements.

🧾 Notes Summary

  • ➡️ Every transaction has a dual effect (debit and credit).
  • ➡️ Assets increase on the debit side, liabilities on the credit.
  • ➡️ The accounting equation: Assets = Liabilities + Capital.
  • ➡️ Prepare a trial balance to verify total debits = total credits.

🧮 Practice Tasks

  1. Record: Owner invests cash ₦100,000 into the business.
  2. Record five more transactions and prepare a trial balance.
  3. Prepare basic financial statements using the trial balance.
